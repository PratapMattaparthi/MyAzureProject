Prevention From Corona Virus

Stay Home, Stay Safe

How To Prevent Virus

Keep Clean Your Objects

It is better to clean the most used objects to kill the virus present on the surfaces. We recommend that if a COVID‑19 case is suspected or confirmed at a facility such as an office or day care, all areas such as offices, bathrooms, common areas, shared electronic equipment like tablets, touch screens, keyboards, remote controls, and ATM machines used by the ill persons should be disinfected

Social Distance

Social distancing prevents the virus infection from one person to another person. The virus transmitted over air also. Hence it is better to maintain distance from everyone. Individuals may apply social distancing methods by staying at home, limiting travel, avoiding crowded areas, and physically distancing themselves from others.

Don't Touch Face

COVID-19 virus can live on some surfaces for several hours. You can get the virus on your hands if you touch a surfaces like gas pump handle, cell phone, a doorknob. Avoid touching any part of your face or head, including your mouth, nose, and eyes. This can give COVID‑19 a chance to go from your hands into your body.

Washing Hands

Washing hands frequently is one of the better ways to kill the virus. It is suggested that cleaning the hands with normal soap also will give better results.

Using Napkin

Use napkin if possible . Use it to clean your sneeze and wash it every time after you used it. It must be only for your own use. Do not share it with anyone.

Wearing Mask

Wear a mask everywhere you go. It prevents the virus from entering into ypur body. Use surgical mask only one time and don't put your mask off and put it on other surfaces as it give chance to virus stick to your mask.
